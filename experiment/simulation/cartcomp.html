Instructions

  1. Choose a value for each attribute in the TRAINING DATASET using the dropdown menus, and then click the ADD button to input these values into the table.
  2. Repeat step 1 until you have a total of 10 rows.
  3. Now, provide a value for each attribute in the TESTING DATASET and then click the ADD button.
  4. Now, click on the NEXT button to initiate the computation phase for the Naive Bayes model.
  5. Please enter the correct number of occurrences for the YES and NO fields based on the OBSERVATION table and click on SUBMIT button.
  6. Now, click on NEXT button to calculate LIKELIHOOD PROBABILITY.
  7. Enter the occurrences of each attribute based on their classes in the YES column of the CALCULATION table, and then click the SUBMIT button.
  8. Repeat step 7 for NO column and click on NEXT button.
  9. If, zero values are present for any likelihood probability, Laplace Smoothing section appear.
  10. Else, calculate the POSTERIOR PROBABILITIES by entering the required fields for P(YES|NEW INSTANCE) and then click on the SUBMIT button.
  11. Repeat the above step for finding P(NO|NEW INSTANCE).
  12. After submitting their values click on NEXT button to know the final result.
